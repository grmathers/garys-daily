Meta Superintelligence Labs Ships Muse Spark, Its First Frontier Model

Meta rolled out Muse Spark, the debut release from Alexandr Wang's Superintelligence Labs and the first in the Muse family. It is a natively multimodal reasoning model with tool use, visual chain-of-thought, and a "contemplating mode" that pits multiple agents against each other on hard problems. Spark scored 52 on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index — fourth overall, trailing Gemini 3.1 Pro, GPT-5.4, and Claude Opus 4.6 — but is notably strong in health reasoning and ships across WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Messenger, and Meta's AI glasses. Wang says the team "rebuilt Meta's AI stack from scratch" in nine months.

Anthropic Opens Claude Managed Agents, a Hosted Platform for Long-Running Agents

Anthropic launched Claude Managed Agents in public beta — a composable API suite that handles the backend plumbing of cloud-hosted agents so teams can go from prototype to production in days.
